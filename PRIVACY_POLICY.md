# Privacy Policy for HighlightText

**Last Updated:** November 25, 2024

## Overview

HighlightText is a Chrome extension that helps you highlight and organize text while reading on the web. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data.**

HighlightText is completely privacy-focused and operates entirely on your local device.

## What Data is Stored Locally

The extension stores your highlights locally in your browser using the built-in localStorage API. This includes:

- Text you've highlighted
- The colors you chose for highlights
- The webpage URLs where highlights are located
- Timestamps of when highlights were created

**Important:** All this data remains on your device only. It is never uploaded to any server or transmitted over the internet.

## Permissions Explained

HighlightText requests the following permissions:

- **Access your data for all websites (`<all_urls>`)**: This permission is required to:
  - Display the highlight toolbar when you select text
  - Apply highlight colors to text on any webpage
  - Save and restore highlights when you revisit pages
  - This does NOT mean we access, read, or collect any data from websites you visit

## Data Security

- All highlights are stored in your browser's localStorage
- No external servers or databases are used
- No analytics or tracking tools are included
- No network requests are made to external services

## Third-Party Services

HighlightText does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or tracking software.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

1. Using the "Clear all" button in the extension
2. Using the keyboard shortcut `Ctrl+Shift+X`
3. Clearing your browser's localStorage
4. Uninstalling the extension

## Children's Privacy

HighlightText does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.
